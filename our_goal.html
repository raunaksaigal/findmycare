Our Goals

If you can think about it for a second, there is Uber for
times when we can’t find a taxi, or Zomato, when we are hungry. There are
online learning platform when we need to learn. So I thought that may be there
could be some platform having the capability to assist us in times of need,
which would contain a centralized database based on your ailments, contain a suggester to find you nearest specialized healthcare units, read from your health reports and suggest may be a better place for your treatments?

How did the project start?


A problem that I faced was related to psychological issue and my family had a tough time with it. Well at first we had a problem finding out a psychiatrist. Even when we found out it didn’t seem to solve the problem. We changed psychiatrist after psychiatrist until we found out about one real near our home who has well experience of the industry. Yet we didn’t find her at first. So that people never have to face such a problem of finding doctors again, I came up with this idea.


There came a time during COVID period when there was a shortage of beds in hospitals, shortage of oxygen, shortage of medicines. If we could have brought in an unified platform which keeps track of medicine, oxygen stocks around us, the beds present in the hospital, and find you the perfect healthcare facility that is also under the budget, then many of us wouldn’t have suffered. This resulted in idea behind creation of the unavailable material service.

Our end goal and the pathway

My idea could also reduce the abnormal charges some
hospitals were taking out of the record for treatment as “donations”. Also as
the number of customer base increases it could factor in the services of the
hospitals into its algorithm and make out suggestions much better. Think of it
as a database of hospitals, treatment facilities, independent doctors and
nursing homes so that you can find the best healthcare unit near you, get
appointments effortlessly, track your healthcare documents and keep them with
you so you don’t get into the hassle of managing paperwork. Also get minor
checkups and tests done at home for you.

Executing the plan

So, now that the problem statement and the end goal of
solution is settled in, we need to figure out the hardest bit – the execution.


At first there has to be some backend, a web interface, and
some server place to fire it up.


We break the procedure in steps:


 - Survey the local hospitals and healthcare units and make a way to incorporate          these units to the database


 -  Survey the problems faced by local people to address their healthcare.


 -  Create a local database of hospitals around and figure out their rating for                   treatment of various ailments based on local inputs and credible sources


 -  Make a list and rank healthcare units from best according to the ailments treated       with success.


 - Compare results and make a list for recommendation of the healthcare unit for a        particular ailment.


 -  Factor in ways to make the treatment better and faster.


 - List out local blood banks, oxygen suppliers, medicine suppliers so that even rare      medicines can be found locally.


 - Find out the people who go to homes for physiotherapy, psychotherapy, drawing     blood, doing ECG, health physicians who visit homes etc. and unify their contacts     in a database.


 -  Connect the persons mentioned above to the persons in need. Also factor in             customer experience with the aforementioned healthcare personnel.


 -  Figure out a way to automate this and hopefully make money out of it.
